Preview of Sema Nami

A gentle bridge between silence and getting help. created for survivors of gender‑based violence.

Why Sema Nami

For moments when speaking out feels impossible.

Many GBV survivors carry their stories silently for months or years. Sema Nami offers a first, gentle step, a place to practice putting feelings into words, understand options, and connect to human support when you are ready.

Trauma‑informed guidance

The chatbot is designed with grounding language, gentle pacing, and content focused on emotional safety, validation, and choice.

Resources in one place

Access curated information on GBV, legal rights, shelters, hotlines, and self‑care tools tailored to your context.

Pathways to therapy

When you feel ready, seamlessly book time with a trauma‑aware therapist for one on one support.

How it works

Three gentle steps to support.

1

Start an anonymous conversation

Share as much or as little as you like with Nami chatbot companion. You can talk about your experiences, emotions, or simply ask questions.

2

Explore tailored resources

Receive information and suggestions based on what you share: grounding exercises, safety planning prompts, legal and medical options, and local support organizations.

3

Connect with a therapist

When you are ready, choose a date and time to speak with a therapist who understands GBV and trauma recovery.

Safe resource hub

Information you can return to, any time.

What the chatbot can help with

  • Understanding GBV and its forms
  • Emotional check‑ins and validation
  • Grounding and breathing exercises
  • Preparing to talk to someone you trust
  • Creating a basic safety plan

What the therapist sessions offer

  • Confidential one on one sessions
  • Trauma‑informed therapeutic support
  • Space to process experiences and emotions
  • Practical coping and healing strategies
  • Support navigating services and systems

Two ways to receive support today.

You can begin with the Nami chatbot, jump straight into a therapist session, or use both together. The pace is yours to choose.
